Simple Future:

The Simple Future tense is a verb form used to express actions that will happen at a later time, or to make predictions, promises, or plans for the future.

Formula:

S+shall/will+v1+O

Example

I will play Tennis.


Future Continuous:

The Future Continuous tense is a verb form used to express actions that will be ongoing or in progress at a specific future time.

Formula:

S+shall/will+be+v4+O

Example

I will be playing Tennis.


Future Perfect:

The Future Perfect tense is a verb form used to express an action that will be completed before a specific point or event in the future.

Formula:

S+shall/will+have+v3+O

Example

I will have played Tennis.


Future Perfect Continuous:

The Future Perfect Continuous tense is a verb form used to express an ongoing action that will continue up to a specific point or event in the future.

Formula:

S+shall/will+have+been+v4+O

Example

I will have been playing Tennis.
